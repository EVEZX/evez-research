## Definition

**Autographenlemnics** (n.) — The science of self-writing, self-referencing logical propositions and the operational order in which they must be evaluated — the PEMDAS of the eigenfield stack.

Every field in the stack produces propositions (lemmas). Every proposition is self-documenting (autographic). Every proposition has a dependency order — you cannot evaluate intervention logic before you have mapping data, just as you cannot compute multiplication before parentheses in arithmetic. Autographenlemnics defines that order.

| Morpheme | Origin | Meaning |
|---------|--------|---------|

| **-graph-** | Greek (graphein) | Writing, inscribing, recording |
| **-en-** | Greek (en) | In, within, into |
| **-lemn-** | Greek (lēmma → lemma) | Proposition, premise, something received/taken for granted |
| **-ics** | Greek (-ikē) | Science, practice, body of knowledge |

Autographenlemnics = the science of self-writing propositions and their operational evaluation order. The propositions are auto-graphic (they write themselves through observation). The propositions are lemmatic (they build on each other as premises). The order is operational (it determines what CAN be computed and what CANNOT until prerequisites are met).

## AEMDAS — The Operational Order

Just as PEMDAS (Parentheses, Exponents, Multiplication, Division, Addition, Subtraction) governs the evaluation order of arithmetic, **AEMDAS** governs the evaluation order of the eigenfield stack.

| Priority | Operation | Field | Mnemonic | What It Does |
|----------|-----------|-------|----------|-------------|
| **A** | **Assert Being** | Ontology | *What IS?* | Establish that spectra exist. No computation without existence. |
| **E** | **Extract Structure** | Eigencartogrophonology + Neuralography | *What IS it?* | Map the spectrum. Record the anatomy. You cannot reason about what you haven't measured. |
| **M** | **Measure Gaps** | Eigenforensics | *What's MISSING?* | Detect the shadows. The gaps are more informative than the presences. |
| **D** | **Deduce Laws** | Nomology + Logic | *What GOVERNS it?* | Derive the nomological code. Determine what interventions are permitted by the system's own laws. |
| **A** | **Assess Interventions** | Interventionalmatonomies + Interspectraloptimetrics | *What CAN change?* | Compute the threat matrix. Calculate coupling, leverage, permeability. Determine the feasible intervention space. |
| **S** | **Speedrun** | Eigenspeedrunning | *What's the OPTIMAL sequence?* | Given the full analysis, compute the minimum-cost, maximum-cascade intervention sequence. Execute. |

### AEMDAS Rules

1. **A before E** — You cannot extract structure from nothing. Establish being first.
2. **E before M** — You cannot measure gaps without a map. The gaps are defined relative to the structure.
3. **M before D** — You cannot deduce laws without knowing what's missing. The shadows reveal the nomology.
4. **D before A** — You cannot assess interventions without knowing the laws. Unlawful intervention is destruction.
5. **A before S** — You cannot speedrun without knowing the feasible space. Blind speedrunning is gambling.
6. **S feeds back to A** — After speedrunning, re-assert being. The intervention changed the system. Begin again.

### Violations and Consequences

| Violation | Skip | Consequence | Real-World Analog |
|-----------|------|-------------|-------------------|
| **E without A** | Extract without asserting | Measuring nothing → garbage data | Analyzing noise |
| **M without E** | Gaps without a map | Can't locate shadows | Blind accusation |
| **D without M** | Laws without gaps | Laws don't account for censorship | Physics without dark matter |
| **A without D** | Intervene without laws | Unpredictable cascade | Surgery without anatomy |
| **S without A** | Speedrun without assessment | Suboptimal or harmful sequence | Random hacking |
| **No feedback** | One-shot, no recursion | System evolved; analysis didn't | Stale intelligence |

## The Metadata Mesh Matrix — Full Specification

### Matrix Dimensions

The mesh matrix M has dimensions N × N where N is the number of lemmas in the stack. Each entry M[i][j] encodes the dependency relationship between lemma i and lemma j.

| Symbol | Relationship | Meaning |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **1** | i → j (direct dependency) | Lemma j requires lemma i as premise |
| **0** | No dependency | Lemmas are independent |
| **-1** | i ← j (reverse dependency) | Lemma i requires lemma j (cycle — forbidden in DAG) |

### Matrix Properties

1. **Upper triangular** — no cycles (DAG property). If M[i][j]=1 then i < j.
2. **Sparse** — most entries are 0. N dependencies for N+1 lemmas. O(N) not O(N²).
3. **Layered** — lemmas cluster into AEMDAS priority levels. Within each level, some parallelism is possible.
4. **Monotone** — information only flows forward through the AEMDAS pipeline. No lemma depends on a later lemma.
5. **Recursive** — REC-8 closes the loop, feeding output back to ONT-0 as new input. The matrix is a spiral, not a line.
