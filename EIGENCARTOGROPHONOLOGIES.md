## Definition

**Eigencartogrophonologies** (n. pl.) — The family of disciplines that map, transcribe, and intervene upon the intrinsic spectral voices of complex systems.

Each system — social, informational, biological, computational — carries an eigenvalue spectrum. That spectrum is not merely descriptive. It is the system's *phonology*: the structural grammar of how information, power, and truth propagate. Eigencartogrophonologies is the study of reading, mapping, and editing that grammar.

## Constituent Subdisciplines

### 1. Eigencartography — The Map
Mapping the spectral landscape of a system. Extracting eigenvalues, identifying clusters, charting the topology of information flow.

**What it produces:** Spectral atlases — the geographic survey of a system's intrinsic structure.

**Implemented in:** IGRE Speedrun genome extraction (`/api/v1/genome/extract`)

### 2. Eigenphonology — The Voice
Reading the frequency signatures of a system. Every eigenvalue is a phoneme — a structural unit of meaning. Negative eigenvalues are the system's silences; positive eigenvalues are its speech. The spectral gap is the prosody.

**What it produces:** Phonetic transcriptions — the grammar of what the system can and cannot say.

**Implemented in:** Consciousness Observatory (Φ, η* tracking), disclosure.tools gap detection

### 3. Eigenforensics — The Evidence
Detecting what has been silenced. Censorship leaves spectral traces — negative eigenvalues, dead links, censor shadows. Eigenforensics reconstructs the missing structure from its acoustic shadow.

**What it produces:** Forensic reports — proofs of absence, gap indices, detectability theorems.

**Implemented in:** disclosure.tools (`:8087`), Eigenforensic Detectability Theorem (>5% redaction detectable at p<0.05)

### 4. Eigenspeedrunning — The Intervention
Finding optimal intervention sequences that reshape the spectral landscape. Not analysis — action. The speedrun computes the minimum-cost sequence of moves that collapses negative eigenvalues and cascades information flow.

**What it produces:** Intervention playbooks — ranked, costed, simulated sequences.

**Implemented in:** IGRE Speedrun (`/api/v1/speedrun/run`)
