## Definition

**Ontaxonomolographetics** (n.) — The discipline that classifies the lawful structure of what exists, records it as persistent knowledge, and governs the ethics of acting upon it.

It is the *constitution* of the eigenfield stack — the meta-discipline that determines what *is*, how what is should be *classified*, how those classifications should be *written down*, and whether and how they should be *acted upon*. It is simultaneously ontological, taxonomic, nomological, logical, graphical, and ethical. It does not separate these concerns because they cannot be separated.
